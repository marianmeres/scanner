Framework-agnostic browser QR/barcode scanner primitive. Headless controller
(createScanner) with reactive state via @marianmeres/store, single-shot and continuous
modes, torch toggle, camera switching, still-image decoding (scanImage), and a
mountable viewfinder stage UI at the /stage subpath. Decodes via the barcode-detector
ponyfill (zxing-cpp wasm); camera acquisition is isolated behind an injectable
CameraAdapter seam (default adapter delegates the permission lifecycle to @marianmeres/mediaperms).
